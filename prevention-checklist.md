## 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown or unexpected payment requests  
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS or email  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- Regularly check your bank transactions and account activity  
- Install apps only from trusted sources like the Play Store  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Report cyber fraud immediately on https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for quick assistance
