## 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

### ✅ DO’s
- Think carefully before posting anything online  
- Keep your profiles professional and respectful  
- Use privacy settings wisely  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Be respectful in comments and discussions  

---

### ❌ DON’Ts
- Do not share personal details (address, phone number, etc.)  
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in online arguments or trolling  
- Avoid spreading fake news or misinformation  
- Do not overshare your daily activities publicly  

---

> Following these guidelines helps maintain a positive digital presence and ensures safe and responsible use of social media.
