##  Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness

For this task, I explored the topic of cybercrime by focusing on UPI (online payment) fraud, which is a common issue in India.

I created a case study explaining how UPI fraud occurs, including the methods used by attackers, the people commonly targeted, and the possible consequences. The case study highlights the importance of being cautious while handling online transactions.

I also prepared a “Stay Safe Online” prevention checklist that includes practical tips for avoiding cyber fraud, along with official reporting channels such as the National Cyber Crime Portal and helpline number 1930.

 The case study and prevention checklist are available in the `task-5-cybercrime/` folder.

> This task helped me understand real-world digital threats and the importance of staying alert and informed while using online platforms.
